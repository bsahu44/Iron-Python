Metric Name Definition Current Step Aging Bucket
MJE Current Step Aging Recs distributed across different Current Step Aging intervals at different MJE Current Steps. [Current Step Aging] : It is the Date Difference in terms of days between [MJE Updated Date] and DateTimeNow() when [MJE Form Status] is not "Approved/Closed"
  • 1 Week : [Current Step Aging]<=7
  • 2 Weeks : ([Current Step Aging]>=8) And ([Current Step Aging]<=14)
  • 3 Weeks : ([Current Step Aging]>=15) And ([Current Step Aging]<=21)
  • 4 Weeks : ([Current Step Aging]>=22) And ([Current Step Aging]<=28)
  • 5 Weeks : ([Current Step Aging]>=29) And ([Current Step Aging]<=35)
  • > 5 Weeks : [Current Step Aging]>35
