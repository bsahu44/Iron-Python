Metric Name Definition KPIs
RTA KPIs KPIs suggesting the count of RTAs at different stage.
  • Total RTA: Total rec count
  • Complete: [Should Be Stage]="Complete"
  • Current Q Target: [Current_Q_T]="Y"
  • Past Due: [RTA_DUE_DATE] < DateTimeNow()
  • On Time: [RTA_DUE_DATE] >= DateTimeNow()
  • Empty: [RTA_DUE_DATE] is NULL
