Metric Name Definition Workflow Aging Bucket
MJE Workflow Aging Recs distributed across different workflow aging intervals at different MJE Form Status. [Workflow Aging] : It is the Date Difference in terms of days between [MJE Logged Date] and DateTimeNow() when [MJE Form Status] is not "Approved/Closed"
  • <= 30 days : [Workflow Aging]<=30
  • 31 - 60 days : ([Workflow Aging]>=31) And ([Workflow Aging]<=60)
  • 61 - 90 days : ([Workflow Aging]>=61) And ([Workflow Aging]<=90)
  • > 90 days : [Workflow Aging]>90
